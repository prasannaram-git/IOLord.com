❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is IOLORD?

IOLORD is a free browser-based gaming portal built by indie developer Prasanna Ram Kulal. Every game on the platform runs instantly inside your browser — zero downloads, zero accounts, zero cost. We focus on crafting original, high-quality HTML5 and WebGL experiences that work across all devices.

What games are available on IOLORD?

Right now, IOLORD features two original titles. RagRush is a 60-level ragdoll obstacle survival platformer where you dodge deadly traps and navigate procedural terrain. DashKill.io is a real-time multiplayer .io arena tournament where one hit eliminates anyone — and every level raises the stakes. Punch Adventure is a premium 2D platformer where a baby monkey fights bully monkeys to protect its toy across 60 levels in 6 zoo worlds — with surprise ground collapses, swinging vines, and atmospheric parallax forests.

Do I need to download anything or sign up?

Not at all. Every game on IOLORD loads directly in your web browser. There are no app installs, no registration forms, and no paywalls. Just open the page and start playing within seconds on any device.

Can I play on my phone or tablet?

Yes — all IOLORD games are designed to be responsive and work across desktops, laptops, Chromebooks, iPhones, iPads, and Android devices. Any modern browser is all you need.

Who is behind IOLORD?

IOLORD was created by Prasanna Ram Kulal, a Computer Science Engineering student at Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bangalore. Originally from Mangalore, Prasanna builds each game from scratch using cutting-edge web technologies — driven by a passion to prove that browser games can rival native experiences.

🌐 Free Online Games at IOLORD

IOLORD exists because we believe gaming should be frictionless. No massive downloads eating your storage. No mandatory sign-ups. No hidden costs. Just open your browser, pick a game, and play — that simple.

Every title on this platform is handcrafted from the ground up using HTML5, WebGL, and Node.js. We obsess over performance, responsiveness, and that "one more try" feeling. Whether you're on a school Chromebook, your work laptop during a break, or a phone on the bus — IOLORD just works.

Currently live: RagRush — a 60-level ragdoll obstacle survival platformer with procedural terrain and deadly traps. And DashKill.io — a real-time multiplayer 1HP arena tournament where every hit counts. More original games are in development.
